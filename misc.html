    Every day I do the crossword on Puzzmo, unlike most other crosswords I've done, these don't usually require you to know the name of every actor/actress or author. Also they can give hints for each clue.

    Speaking of crosswords, eventually I hope to graduate to cryptic crosswords. Minute Cryptic is a single cryptic crossword puzzle to solve each day and even has video explanations of how you could arrive at the solution. They even have a guide on how to solve regular cryptic puzzles so I would definitely recommend checking it out if you like wordplay.
