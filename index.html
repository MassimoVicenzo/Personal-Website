I am currently a graduate student at the University of Waterloo studying at the Combinatorics and Optimization department, under the supervision of Prof. Luke Postle.

My research interests lie mostly in the extremal combinatorics area and graph theory. More specifically, I enjoy problems in graph coloring, extremal graph minor theory, planarity and Ramsey numbers, but I will almost always be interested in anything with discrete objects or an extremal flavor. Moreover, I've dabbled in proof formalization; using LEAN to prove theorems in graph theory.

Outside of math, some of my interests are tabletop RPGs (I am the forever DM), video games and miniature painting (WH40k Necrons) — the archetypical nerdy hobbies.
